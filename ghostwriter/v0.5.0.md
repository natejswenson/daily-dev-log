## What Shipped

Ghostwriter builds the visual cards that go with a LinkedIn post, and v0.5.0
adds a new one: a matrix card. It is a comparison grid, rows and columns,
for the posts where the point is options measured against each other rather
than a single flow or a list. It joins the existing card family alongside the
ramp, flow, terminal, and carousel types.

The pattern by now is steady: a post shape shows up often enough that it earns
its own card, so it gets one and the vocabulary grows by one.

## What's Next

The card library keeps expanding as new post shapes call for their own
treatment.

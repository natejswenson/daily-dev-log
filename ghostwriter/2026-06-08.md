## What I Built

Gave the ghostwriter a new way to show momentum. The **ramp card** renders three ascending steps with the final one highlighted — the right shape for a post about an accelerating progression (growth curves, adoption, a streak that's compounding). The bars are deliberately illustrative rather than to-scale; the labeled figures carry the truth, so the visual reads as honest at a glance instead of implying precision it doesn't have.

It's a new card template plus its `.card.ramp` styles, wired into the skill's Visuals guidance so the model knows when to reach for it. The feature had been stranded on an unmerged branch from before the skills were consolidated; I brought it into the monorepo cleanly through a pull request, with the skill's scorer and full test suite green on the way in.

## What's Next

Use the ramp card on a real post to pressure-test the proportions, and see whether the same "illustrative, not to scale" treatment is worth generalizing to the other card types.

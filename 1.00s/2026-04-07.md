## What I Built

### Arcade Zen Flow Redesign

Ripped out the old lives-based arcade system and replaced it with a zen flow — instant retry, no lives, atmospheric progression. The old system punished exploration; the new one rewards persistence. Added direct entry (skip the countdown), hit feedback effects, death moment polish, and post-run summary hooks.

### Arcade Lobby

Built a competitive pre-game lobby screen from scratch, then iterated it through 6 versions. Started with a basic "BEAT player123" screen, fixed duplicate PLAY buttons and orb sizing, added a top nav bar with sci-fi medals, scaled up typography, added animations with podium platforms and a glowing CTA, and fixed a bug where the lobby showed 0 when leaderboard data hadn't synced to the local store yet.

### Daily Challenge Lobby

Created a polished pre-game lobby for daily challenges with custom type icons and difficulty display. Fixed a bug where the type shown didn't match the actual challenge, scaled up the hero icon, and wired the WATCH AD button to properly present the ad and grant +5 attempts.

### Mode Navigation Icons

Replaced placeholder mode icons with custom energy sphere orbs. Went through 6 iterations — started with wireframe concepts (infinity symbol, concentric rings), moved to layered gradient treatments, then proper mini SciFiLevelOrb spheres, then pure energy spheres with no ring frame, and finally a production-polished crisp version. The key lesson: mini versions of the existing orb looked better than clever abstract icons.

### Loop Map — Procedural Background

Replaced the blurry 1080×12900px JPEG map background with an 11-layer procedural rendering system. Layers include: indigo gradient base, hex-cell grid, section-colored radial washes, 700-star field with 55 feature stars and cross-flares, Canvas-drawn organic nebula clusters, arena glow halos, interstitial fill clouds, boss ring echoes, vertical dust columns, diagonal light rays, and edge vignettes. Sharp at any zoom level, and unlocked sections glow brighter than locked ones.

### Loop Map — 50-Iteration Polish

Ran 50+ focused iterations across the entire map UI. Highlights: locked orbs grew from 62pt to 70pt, section labels got progress bars, energy conduits got brighter and wider, boss gates show two-line labels with shadows, initial scroll centers on the next-to-play level, confirmation panels became lightweight glass cards sitting below the selected orb, and the PLAY button matches section color with a glow. Removed the redundant level bar overlay entirely.

## What's Next

The three game modes (loop, arcade, daily) now all have polished lobbies and distinct visual identities. Next focus is playtesting the full progression across all 50 levels and tuning difficulty curves.

## What I Built

Big architectural shift today. I redesigned the arcade competition system from the ground up — moving from multiple modes to a single focused arcade experience built around weekly leaderboard cycles. The idea is to keep things simple and competitive: one mode, one leaderboard, weekly resets.

I stripped out the daily streak tracking from `ArcadeStatsStore` since it no longer fits the weekly competition model. Instead, the focus is on weekly performance and climbing the leaderboard.

Also built a weekly share card so players can share their results at the end of each competition cycle. This ties into the social loop — play the week, see where you land, share your score, get friends to compete.

## What's Next

These changes are on a feature branch and need testing before merging to main. Next step is verifying the weekly reset logic and making sure the leaderboard UI feels right with the new competition cycle.
